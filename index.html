FAQ

Why are my emails hitting spam even though I just got my domain?
Your new domain is “cold”. Proper SPF/DKIM/DMARC setup and gradual sending help your emails reach the inbox reliably.
Can you guarantee I will get more sales?
Results depend on copy, list quality, and campaign strategy. We ensure emails land in the inbox where they can actually be seen.
Why aren’t I getting any clicks on my current emails?
Emails may be landing in spam or promotions, or messaging isn’t resonating. Warm inbox + template guidance helps engagement.
Do I have to warm my domain before sending campaigns?
Yes. Gradual sending and monitoring builds reputation, preventing spam filtering and improving open rates.
What exactly is “warm delivery”?
Ensuring emails land safely and predictably in the inbox, like a stork delivering to the right doorstep.
